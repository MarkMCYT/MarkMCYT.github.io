FAQ

Do the drones come with a warranty?

Yes! All drones purchased through our website come with a one-year basic warranty, though this can be
extended to a two-year extended warranty for $49.99 or a three-year extended warranty for $99.99.

What determines the prices?

The pricing is determined by the cost for us to acquire the drone plus a small markup to allow us
to pay for the servers running this website.

How long does it take to deliver?

We do not handle the delivery, but if you are located in or around the city of Hamilton, Ontario it should
only take 1-2 business days.
